CadenceCloud

CadenceCloud is a personal music organization, streaming, and hosting service. We want your music to be easy to access from anywhere, organized automatically (but with you in full control), and to be played at the best quality possible.

Features

Upload any music you have

CadenceCloud allows you to listen to your music from anywhere. Simply upload music, and we'll take care of everything else.

Organize your entire music library

When you upload music, CadenceCloud can automatically sort through it and fix metadata. It can also ask you about music it can't find.

Play your music anywhere

CadenceCloud lets you stream your music to any device with a modern browser.

Save music offline

Never worry about patchy connections or dead zones. CadenceCloud can download music to your device so that you can listen no matter what condition your WiFi is in.
